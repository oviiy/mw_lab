HOW TO PLAY

Paint materials by dragging on the world. Every cell has a real temperature and heat flows between materials — so reactions emerge on their own.

• HEAT / COOL tools raise or lower temperature — light fires or freeze lakes with no matter at all.
• Pour LAVA into water to forge obsidian and boil steam.
• Melt SAND into glass, melt and re-forge METAL, burn the forest, set off gunpowder.
• Toggle 🌡 HEAT view to see the world as a thermal camera.
• Complete objectives to earn XP, level up, and unlock new materials.
